## Packages
framer-motion | Essential for the smooth scroll animations, entry animations, and hover effects requested for the premium agency feel.
react-intersection-observer | Useful helper for triggering animations when elements scroll into view.
lucide-react | Already in stack, but explicitly noting its use for icons in services and solutions sections.
react-hook-form | For handling the contact form state efficiently.
@hookform/resolvers | For connecting Zod schemas to react-hook-form.

## Notes
The application relies heavily on Framer Motion for its premium feel.
The primary call to action is WhatsApp, but a contact form is also implemented to utilize the provided backend inquiries API.
Background colors and neon glows use custom Tailwind configurations or raw CSS variables defined in index.css.
